# Lesson 28: Presenting New Products or Fabrics

**Level:** B2 (Upper Intermediate - Advanced)  
**Students:** 8 adult learners  
**Duration:** 60–75 minutes  
**Focus:** Describing qualities, specifications, pricing, benefits – specifically for fabric/product buyers

---

## **Stage 1: Warm-up & Lead-in (10 min)**

### Teacher Script:
"Today we're focusing on presenting products – especially fabrics – to potential buyers. This is crucial for sales, trade fairs, and client meetings!"

**Warm-up Questions:**
1. Do you sell products? What's challenging about presenting them?
2. What information do buyers want to know?
3. What makes a product presentation persuasive?
4. How do you describe quality in English?

*Discuss 4 minutes.*

---

## **Stage 2-8: [Following standard structure]**

### **Key Language:**

**DESCRIBING FABRIC/PRODUCT QUALITIES:**

**1. Material & Composition:**
- "This is made from 100% organic cotton."
- "It's a blend of 60% linen, 40% cotton."
- "The composition is..."
- "It contains..."

**2. Texture & Feel:**
- "The fabric is soft/smooth/crisp/breathable."
- "It has a luxurious feel."
- "Notice the quality when you touch it."
- "It's lightweight but durable."

**3. Technical Specifications:**
- "The weight is 150 GSM (grams per square meter)."
- "The width is 150 cm."
- "Thread count: 200."
- "It's available in 20 colors."

**4. Performance Features:**
- "It's wrinkle-resistant."
- "It's machine washable at 40 degrees."
- "It doesn't shrink."
- "It has excellent color fastness."
- "It's moisture-wicking."

**5. Certifications & Standards:**
- "It's OEKO-TEX certified."
- "It meets EU safety standards."
- "It's certified organic."
- "It's sustainably produced."

**6. Pricing:**
- "The price is $15 per meter."
- "For quantities over 1000 meters, we offer..."
- "This includes..."
- "Compared to similar products, this offers better value because..."

**7. Availability & Lead Time:**
- "We have this in stock."
- "Lead time is 4-6 weeks."
- "Minimum order quantity is 500 meters."
- "We can provide samples."

**Practice:** Students present a fabric/product using all these elements.

*Product presentation role-plays – 15 minutes.*
