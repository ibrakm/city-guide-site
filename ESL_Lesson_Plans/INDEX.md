## 💡 **Quick Tips**

### Teaching Tips:
- ✅ Read entire lesson before class
- ✅ Follow timing guidelines (use timer!)
- ✅ Maximize student speaking time (60-70%)
- ✅ Use pair/group work frequently
- ✅ Monitor all activities actively
- ✅ Correct errors gently during fluency tasks
- ✅ Praise all communication attempts

### Adaptation Tips:
- 🔄 Adjust timing based on your class speed
- 🔄 Modify examples to your local context
- 🔄 Add/remove activities based on class size
- 🔄 Adapt homework to your center's policy
- 🔄 Use technology when available (videos, apps)

### Differentiation Tips:
- 📊 Weaker students: More scaffolding, simpler patterns
- 📊 Average students: Follow curriculum as written
- 📊 Advanced students: High achiever challenges in every lesson
