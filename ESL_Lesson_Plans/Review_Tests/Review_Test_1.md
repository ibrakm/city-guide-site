# Review Test 1: Unit 1 (Lessons 1-6)

**Level**: A1  
**Duration**: 60 minutes  
**Target Group**: 12-14 Moroccan students (6th-9th grade)  
**Content**: Colors, Numbers 1-100, Classroom Objects, Days/Months, Weather/Seasons  

---

## TEST OVERVIEW

This review test assesses students' mastery of Unit 1 content (Lessons 1-6). The test includes:
- Listening comprehension
- Vocabulary recognition and production
- Reading comprehension
- Writing tasks
- Speaking/oral assessment

**Total Points**: 100 points  
**Passing Score**: 60/100

---

## TEACHER INSTRUCTIONS

### Before the Test (5 minutes)

**Teacher says:**
"Good morning, everyone! Today we have Review Test 1. This test covers everything we learned in Lessons 1 through 6: colors, numbers, classroom objects, days, months, weather, and seasons. 

The test has different sections:
- Listening (I will read something, you answer)
- Vocabulary (matching, completing)
- Reading (read a text, answer questions)
- Writing (write sentences)
- Speaking (oral questions)

Do your best! Use everything you learned. You have 45 minutes for the written part, then 15 minutes for oral assessments. Are you ready? Good luck! You may begin."

### Test Administration Tips:
- Read listening sections TWICE, slowly and clearly
- Walk around during written sections to monitor (but don't help!)
- For oral section, call students one by one or in pairs
- Create a calm, supportive testing environment
- Remind students to write clearly and check their work

---

## WRITTEN TEST (45 minutes, 80 points)

### SECTION A: LISTENING COMPREHENSION (15 points)

**Teacher Instructions:**
"Section A is listening. I will read a short text twice. Listen carefully. Then answer the questions. Let me read the text first."

#### Listening Text (Teacher reads TWICE, slowly and clearly):

```
"Hello! My name is Karim. I am fourteen years old. My birthday is on March 
15th. I love the color blue. I have a blue school bag and a blue pencil case. 
In my bag, I have five notebooks, three pens, two pencils, and one ruler. 
Today is Monday. It's sunny and warm outside. Monday is my favorite day 
because I have English class. My favorite season is spring because the weather 
is beautiful. In spring, it's not too hot and not too cold. Perfect!"
```

**Teacher says:**
"Now answer these questions. Write your answers on your paper."

#### Questions (3 points each):

1. How old is Karim?
2. When is Karim's birthday?
3. What is Karim's favorite color?
4. How many notebooks does Karim have?
5. What day is it in the story?

**ANSWER KEY:**
1. He is fourteen years old. / Fourteen.
2. March 15th / March fifteenth.
3. Blue.
4. Five notebooks. / Five.
5. Monday.

### SECTION D: WEATHER & SEASONS (10 points)

#### Part 1: Match Season to Description (4 points, 1 point each)

**Teacher says:**
"Match the season to the description."

1. Spring → a) Very hot, no rain
2. Summer → b) Cold, sometimes snowy
3. Autumn → c) Warm, flowers bloom
4. Winter → d) Cool, leaves fall

**ANSWER KEY:**
1. c
2. a
3. d
4. b

#### Part 2: Complete Weather Sentences (6 points, 1 point each)

**Teacher says:**
"Complete these sentences with weather words."

1. In summer, it's very __________ in Morocco.
2. When it's __________, I need an umbrella.
3. It's __________ today. I can see the sun!
4. In winter, it's __________ at night.
5. The __________ is blowing strongly today!
6. I love __________ days because the weather is nice!

**ANSWER KEY:**
1. hot / sunny
2. rainy / raining
3. sunny
4. cold
5. wind
6. sunny / warm

### SECTION E: READING COMPREHENSION (10 points)

**Teacher says:**
"Read this text carefully. Then answer the questions below."

#### Reading Text:

```
My School Day

My name is Sara. I am thirteen years old. I go to school from Monday to 
Friday. My school bag is red and black. Inside, I have seven books, a green 
pencil case, a ruler, and an eraser.

Today is Wednesday. It's cloudy and cool outside. I'm wearing a blue jacket. 
Wednesday is a busy day for me. I have six classes: math, Arabic, French, 
English, science, and art. My favorite class is English!

After school, I go home and do homework. I have a lot of homework on 
Wednesday! My birthday is in November, in the autumn. I will be fourteen 
years old. I can't wait!
```

#### Questions (2 points each):

1. What color is Sara's school bag?
2. What day is it in the story?
3. What's the weather like on Wednesday?
4. How many classes does Sara have on Wednesday?
5. When is Sara's birthday?

**ANSWER KEY:**
1. Red and black.
2. Wednesday.
3. Cloudy and cool.
4. Six classes.
5. In November. / November.

### SECTION F: WRITING (10 points)

**Teacher says:**
"Write 5 sentences about yourself. Use the topics we learned. Write clearly!"

**Topics to include:**
1. Your age
2. Your favorite color
3. What day it is today
4. Your favorite season
5. One thing in your school bag

**EXAMPLE:**
```
I am fourteen years old. My favorite color is green. Today is Monday. My 
favorite season is summer because it's hot and sunny. In my school bag, I 
have six notebooks.
```

**GRADING RUBRIC:**
- 2 points per sentence (grammar, vocabulary, content)
- Deduct 0.5 points for minor errors
- Deduct 1 point for major errors or missing information

## ORAL ASSESSMENT (15 minutes, 20 points)

**Teacher Instructions:**
After students complete the written test, call them individually or in pairs for oral assessment. Each student should take 2-3 minutes.

### Oral Questions (20 points total, 4 points each)

**Teacher asks each student 5 questions from this list:**

#### Questions Bank:

1. **Colors & Objects:**
   - "What color is your pen/bag/shirt today?"
   - "Name 5 colors in English."
   - "What's your favorite color? Why?"

2. **Numbers:**
   - "Count from 1 to 20."
   - "Count from 20 to 50 by tens."
   - "How old are you?"
   - "How many students are in this class?"

3. **Classroom Objects:**
   - "Name 5 things in your school bag."
   - "What do you use to write?"
   - "What do you use to erase mistakes?"

4. **Days & Months:**
   - "What day is today? What day was yesterday?"
   - "Say the days of the week in order."
   - "What month is it now?"
   - "When is your birthday?"

5. **Weather & Seasons:**
   - "What's the weather like today?"
   - "What's your favorite season? Why?"
   - "What do you do in summer?"
   - "Describe the weather in winter in Morocco."

### Oral Grading Rubric (per question, 4 points):

- **4 points**: Perfect answer, clear pronunciation, full sentence
- **3 points**: Good answer, minor errors, mostly clear
- **2 points**: Basic answer, some errors, understandable
- **1 point**: Attempted answer, many errors, difficult to understand
- **0 points**: No answer or completely incorrect

## AFTER THE TEST: REVIEW SESSION (Optional, can be done in next class)

### Teacher Script for Reviewing Answers

**Teacher says:**
"Let's review the test together! I will go through each section and give you the correct answers. Check your work and learn from any mistakes!"

### Review Tips:

1. **Go through each section together**
2. **Explain common mistakes**
3. **Have students repeat correct answers orally**
4. **Celebrate successes - praise students who did well!**
5. **Offer extra help to students who scored below 60**

### Common Errors to Review:

**Colors:**
- Confusing color names
- Spelling errors (e.g., "bleu" instead of "blue")

**Numbers:**
- Thirteen vs. thirty, fourteen vs. forty
- Forgetting hyphens in compound numbers (forty-five, not forty five)

**Days/Months:**
- Spelling Wednesday correctly
- Remembering capital letters

**Weather:**
- Using "It's" correctly (It's sunny, not "Is sunny")
- Confusing adjectives and verbs (sunny vs. raining)
